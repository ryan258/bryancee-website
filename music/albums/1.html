Blue Bird Delux

Released: 5/15/2024

Blue Bird Delux is a soaring journey through the skies of consciousness, inviting listeners to spread their wings and embrace the freedom of self-discovery.

Behind the Scenes

The inspiration for Blue Bird Delux came during a transformative hiking trip in the Andes. Surrounded by the majesty of nature and the endless blue sky, I felt a profound connection to the world around me and the limitless potential within each of us.
